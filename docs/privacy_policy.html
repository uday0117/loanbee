Privacy Policy for LoanBee

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Introduction

UK Solutions ("us", "we", or "our") operates the LoanBee mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information.

LoanBee is a financial calculator application that performs calculations locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Store your calculation history
  • Transmit data to our servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Require account registration
  • Request any permissions beyond what's necessary for the app to function

Data Storage

All calculations are performed locally on your device. No calculation data is stored permanently or transmitted to any server. The app only stores your theme preference (dark/light mode) locally on your device.

Currency Conversion Data

The currency converter feature fetches live exchange rates from a public API (exchangerate-api.com) to provide accurate conversions. This request does not include any personal information and only retrieves publicly available exchange rate data.

Third-Party Services

LoanBee uses minimal third-party services:

  • Exchange Rate API: Used only for fetching currency conversion rates. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Google Fonts: May be used for displaying text (loaded from device cache when available).

These services do not collect information used to identify you.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
